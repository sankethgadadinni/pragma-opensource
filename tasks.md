# Tasks

## Credit scoring

> “Based on this customer’s history, how risky are they?”

Usually the output is a probability of default or a credit-risk score used for lending, limits, or approvals. (arxiv.org)

---

## Fraud detection

> “Does this transaction or recent behavior look suspicious?”

The output is a fraud score or alert so the bank can block, review, or step-up authenticate. (arxiv.org)

---

## Communication engagement

> “If we send this customer a push/email/SMS, are they likely to engage?”

This is basically marketing/CRM prediction: open, click, respond, or otherwise interact. (arxiv.org)

---

## Recurrent transaction detection

> “Is this part of a repeating pattern?”

Example: salary deposits, rent, Netflix, gym, utility bills. This helps identify subscriptions, recurring expenses, and recurring income. (arxiv.org)

---

## Lifetime value prediction

> “How valuable will this customer be in the future?”

Usually this means predicting future revenue, balances, retention, or total business value from that customer. (arxiv.org)

---

## Product recommendation

> “What should we offer this customer next?”

Example: premium plan, savings product, loan, insurance, trading, or some other banking product. This is basically next-best-product prediction. (arxiv.org)
